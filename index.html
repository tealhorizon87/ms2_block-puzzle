How to Play

Controls

  • You can use either the arrow or WSAD keys
  • Use the left-right/A-D keys to move the block sideways
  • Down-S moves the block faster
  • Up-W rotates the block

Rules

  • Clear as many lines as possible by fitting the blocks together until a whole row is full
  • Earn points for every line you make:
    • 10 for one line
    • 50 for two line
    • 150 for three line
  • The 'next block' window will help you plan your moves
  • If the blocks get too high, the game is over
